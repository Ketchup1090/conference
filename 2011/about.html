About

The Ruby Community in India, together with the Innovation & Technology Trust, is pleased to announce RubyConf India 2011 on May 28th and 29th at the Royal Orchid Hotel, Bangalore. Targeted at Ruby practitioners and anyone remotely interested or curious about Ruby, it will offer Ruby enthusiasts an opportunity to brainstorm, network and learn about the latest technology changes first-hand.

Being the second RubyConf to be hosted in India, this 2-day event will feature prominent speakers like Chad Fowler, author of “The Passionate Programmer”, and Ola Bini, core-committer to JRuby since 2006.

Ruby is dynamic programming language that offers an ideal development environment for Agile practitioners. It has one of the most active open source communities worldwide which produces and supports tools and projects like “Ruby on Rails” - a powerful web application development framework that works well on different platforms, and significantly reduces time-to-market and operating costs.

A strong supporter of open source technologies that promise to revolutionise the IT landscape, ThoughtWorks is the Platinum and Technology Sponsor for this event, and is supported by Ruby Central – an international non-profit dedicated to the advocacy of the Ruby programming language across the globe.
