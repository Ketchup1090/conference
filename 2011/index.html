RubyConf India 2011

India's second RubyConf is happening in Bangalore in May 2011. RubyConf India is presented by the Ruby Community in India and supported by RubyCentral and the Innovation & Technology Trust.
Royal Orchid Hotel, Bangalore
28th & 29th May

Sponsorship Opportunities

If you would like to be a sponsor at RubyConf India 2011, you can download the sponsorship kit and mail us your choice at sponsors@rubyconfindia.org

Participation Information

The Call for Speakers for RubyConf India 2011 is now open.

If you want to submit a proposal, kindly mail it to proposals@rubyconfindia.org along with your bio (plain text only, please) with the following information:

About you
  • Name
  • Nationality
  • Contact number
About your talk
  • Title
  • Detailed description (2 or 3 paragraphs)
  • Target audience (newbies, advanced, etc.)

Proposals received will be reviewed by a panel consisting of Ola Bini, core-committer to JRuby since 2006, and Chad Fowler, author of "The Passionate Programmer" and "My Job went to India."

Watch this space (or follow us on twitter) for announcements regarding accepted proposals and registration for the event. Accepted proposals will be announced by the end of March, and registrations will open in the first week of April.
