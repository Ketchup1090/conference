Terms and Conditions

Room Sharing

  1. Room sharing is strictly on person of same gender.
  2. After booking tickets - please send an email to stay@rubyconfindia.org if you have formed a group to share the room and mention the names of ticket holders.
  3. If you are not part of any group, you will be automatically allotted rooms with people of same gender, 3 days prior to the conference.
  4. EVERYONE staying at the hotel is required to carry original proof of identity, without which accommodation will be denied!

Hotel expenses and transfers

  1. All additional expenses must be borne by the attendees when you check-out.
  2. Expenses towards non-complimentary hotel amenities, room-service and mini-bar usage will be borne by the attendee.
  3. it is impossible for us to manage the transfers for all guests. Please arrange airport / railway station transfers by yourself or co-ordinate with the hotel for this. This expense will be borne by attendees.

Refunds and Transfers

  1. Tickets are refundable till 30th Jan, 2014. Please send a mail to refunds@rubyconfindia.org
  2. After 30th Jan, tickets are non-refundable but transferable. Please mention explicitly whom you are transferring tickets to by sending an email to refunds@rubyconfindia.org.

For any other questions or clarifications, refer to our FAQ or drop us a note at team@rubyconfindia.org.
